# Soft-Summer-color-palette
Soft Summer color palette belongs to Soft Summe, that is one of the four sub-seasons in the Summer family of the 16-season colour analysis framework. It sits at the most muted, most blended corner of the Summer family — sharing its cool undertone with True Summer and Light Summer, borrowing its greyed-down quality from its neighbour Soft Autumn.
This data has been gathered from and is present in more details on https://softsummerpalette.com
